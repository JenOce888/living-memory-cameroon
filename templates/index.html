About this project

This application was built to preserve knowledge that lives only in the memory of elders. It collects three types of data:

Historical testimonies

Personal accounts of events the elders witnessed: independence, reunification, armed conflicts, social changes.

Oral traditions

Myths, legends, proverbs, folktales, and cultural rituals transmitted across generations through the spoken word.

Medicinal plants

Traditional botanical knowledge: plant names in local languages, diseases treated, preparation methods, and cultural context.
